Industry Exhibition

Invitation for Exhibition & Sponsorship

The 7th IEEE International Conference on Industrial Cyber-Physical Systems (ICPS) is a platform for researchers and pioneers in industrial technology to converge, discuss, and drive the transformation of cyber-physical systems in various industries. This year's program boasts a lineup of esteemed speakers, cutting-edge research presentations, and interactive sessions that delve into the most pressing challenges and exciting opportunities in the field.

Why Sponsor or Exhibit?

Seize the opportunity to showcase your organization's commitment to innovation by becoming a sponsor or exhibitor at our conference. As a sponsor, you will gain unparalleled visibility among industry professionals, researchers, and decision-makers. Exhibit your products and services to a targeted audience actively seeking the latest advancements in industrial cyber-physical systems.
