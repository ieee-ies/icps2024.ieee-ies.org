Conference Registration

Please read the instruction below carefully before process for your registration.


  • In order to participate in the conference of ICPS 2024, at least one author of each paper must register for the conference. Only papers associated with author registration can be included in the program.
  • Each regular paper author registration may cover up to two papers, and each extended abstract author registration may cover up to one paper.
  • Non-author registration includes access to the full conference, including the workshops, special events, tutorials, coffee breaks, lunches, welcome reception, but does not include paper registration and Gala-dinner.
  • Each additional paper incurs a fee of USD 250 per paper, limited to a maximum of two additional papers. The page limit is 6 pages. Each additional page incurs a fee of USD 100 per page.
